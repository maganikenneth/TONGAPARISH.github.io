President Uhuru Kenyatta Joins the Bishops in planting trees at Marian Shrine – Subukia Nakuru on 22nd February 2020

President Uhuru supports Bishops’ war on corruption

His Excellency President Uhuru Kenyatta on 22nd, February 2020 joined the Catholic Bishops in intensifying war against corruption at Marian Shrine – Subukia in Nakuru Diocese. Speaking during the launch of this year’s Lenten Campaign, President Kenyatta echoed the Bishops call for the individual examination of conscience and the need to start the war against corruption at a personal level. “Every Kenyan has a responsibility to fight against corruption. “Do not point fingers at the others.” He said adding that people also have to take care of the environment.
